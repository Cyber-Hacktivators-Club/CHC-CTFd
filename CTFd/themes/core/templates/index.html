Instructions

Registration Process

All participants must register through the official event website. Ensure that each team member is registered individually and linked to their respective teams. Registration closes 24 hours before the event start.

Accessing Challenges

Once the CTF starts, challenges will be accessible through the participant dashboard. Challenges vary in difficulty, and each provides a specific number of points based on its complexity.

Communication Channels

Participants will have access to an official communication channel for asking questions or seeking clarifications regarding challenges. This platform is moderated, and all interactions should adhere to the code of conduct.

Scoring and Leaderboard

Points are awarded for each correctly solved challenge. The leaderboard updates in real-time and displays team rankings throughout the event. Monitor it to see where your team stands!






Rules

General Conduct

Participants must conduct themselves in a respectful and collegiate manner at all times. Disruptive behavior, use of unauthorized tools, or cheating is strictly prohibited and will result in disqualification.

Flag Submission

Flags must be submitted in the CHC{} format. Each correct flag earns points, and points are deducted for incorrect submissions. Ensure your submissions are precise to avoid penalties.

Teamwork

Teams are limited to a maximum of 4 participants. Collaboration within teams is encouraged; however, sharing solutions between different teams is not allowed and will be banned in this case as the flags are DYNAMIC.
